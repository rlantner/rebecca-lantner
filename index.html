About Me

I'm a data-viz nerd based in Cambridge, MA.

Hi! I'm Rebecca.

I find beauty in data.

On the job: I'm a senior quantitative data analyst at Jobcase, Inc. I write complex SQL scripts and construct multivariate tests to optimize email content recommended to our members. I build dashboards to communicate results, focusing on actionable business insights that move key metrics.

On the side: I'm a data artist. I delight in the layer of creativity that transforms a query into a striking, data-driven experience. From sketchbook to spreadsheet, I employ a range of tools to design and build intuitive data stories and visualizations.
